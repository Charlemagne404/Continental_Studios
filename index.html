About

Continental is my personal umbrella brand, a home for the digital work I build, experiment with, and polish until it feels real. Websites, tools, games, bots, and even a custom Linux setup all live under the same identity, while each project keeps its own name and style. If you see the globe mark or “A Continental Studios production,” that is the signature.
